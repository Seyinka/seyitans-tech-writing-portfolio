[Mautic](https://www.mautic.org/) is an open-source **marketing automation platform** that helps businesses manage campaigns, segment contacts, and track user engagement across multiple channels.

## Overview

Mautic empowers marketing teams to:

- Automate personalized campaigns.
- Capture and segment leads.
- Integrate with CRMs, CMSs, and email services.
- Analyze engagement metrics with detailed reporting.

## Key Features

- **Contact Management:** Collect and store contact details, track interactions, and segment audiences.
- **Campaign Automation:** Build workflows with triggers and conditions.
- **Email Marketing:** Send and schedule targeted email campaigns.
- **Landing Pages & Forms:** Create forms and pages to capture leads.
- **REST API:** Connect Mautic to external apps and services.

## Target Audience

- Digital marketers
- Developers integrating marketing tools
- Startups or SMEs looking for open-source alternatives to HubSpot or Marketo
